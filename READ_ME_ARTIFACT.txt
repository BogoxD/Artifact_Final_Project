NAVIGATION
To navigate on play mode use:
WASD for movement
Middle Mouse Button press to rotate camera

STEERING
In order to move units move the slide that's at the bottom of the FormationHandler script in the inspector. FormationHandler script is a component attached for every formation.

FORMATION PARAMETERS
In order to tweek the formation parameters, go in the inspector tab and look for each formation's geometric script. For example Box Formation. All the modified variables are shown in the inspector.
